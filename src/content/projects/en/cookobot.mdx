## AI-Powered Assistant Robot

Robotic system integrating **ROS**, **computer vision** with TensorFlow, and **web interface** for kitchen assistance.

### Cross-Platform Ecosystem

- **ROS (Robot Operating System)**: Robot control and coordination
- **VueJS + Bulma**: Responsive web interface for remote control
- **TensorFlow**: Visual recognition of ingredients and utensils
- **Python Backend**: Business logic and real-time communication
