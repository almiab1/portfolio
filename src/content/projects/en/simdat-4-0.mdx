## Industrial Digital Transformation

Project focused on **implementing Industry 4.0 technologies** in real manufacturing environments, developing demonstrators incorporating IoT, Virtual Reality, Digital Twins, Additive Manufacturing, and RFID.

### My Role

As a **developer**, I participated in implementing multiple technological demonstrators integrating various Industry 4.0 technologies.

### Implemented Technologies

#### Simulation and Digital Twins
- **Industrial process simulation**: Virtual visualization and analysis of robotic cell operations
- **Virtual Reality**: Generation of Digital Twins to replicate real operations virtually
- **Optimization**: Identification of improvements and optimization of operational parameters

#### Internet of Things (IoT)
- **Real-time monitoring**: Data collection on control and status of industrial processes
- **Quality control**: Automatic analysis of defects in produced parts
- **Decision making**: Pattern and anomaly analysis for performance improvements

#### Additive Manufacturing
- **Advanced molds**: Mold manufacturing through 3D printing
- **Conformal Cooling**: Advanced cooling techniques to optimize injection cycles
- **Quality improvement**: Reduction of deformations and uniform cooling

#### RFID and Traceability
- **Material location**: Traceability of recyclable material using RFID technology
- **Waste management**: Optimization of material management and location
- **Sustainability**: Promotion of circular economy from the manufacturing process
